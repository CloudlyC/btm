Back Walking Massage

Price: $60/$70/$80 - 30/45/60 minutes

Description: Experience the unique benefits of Back Walking Massage, also known as Ashiatsu, where the therapist uses their feet to deliver deep, consistent pressure along your back and other areas of the body. This technique provides more substantial pressure compared to traditional hand-based massage, making it exceptionally effective for releasing deep-seated tension and muscle knots.

Ideal for those who prefer a strong, firm touch, this massage promotes deep relaxation, enhances circulation, and improves posture. By targeting and alleviating persistent muscle tightness, Back Walking Massage helps you achieve a profound sense of ease and balance.
